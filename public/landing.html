Online vs aapka mohalla

🌐 Online apps
Online bas vo milega
jo store ke paas hai

Quick-commerce ho ya e-commerce — stock wahi dikhega jo unke warehouse mein hai. Indian swadeshi buyers ki asli zaroorat ye bade Babu log match nahi kar paate.

✕ Limited warehouse stock
✕ "Unavailable" ya e-commerce wali 2–7 din ki delivery — wahi product 5 km door dukaan mein mil sakta hai
✕ Fixed service timing — emergency mein trust karna mushkil
📍 Dukanchi
Offline sab milega
jo aapke 🫵 aas-paas hai

Ab dukaan-dukaan jaakar dhoondhna nahi padega. Dukanchi app par local retailer se chat karo aur seedhe store se buy karo.

✓ Pura local bazaar ek jagah
✓ Product search karo, nearby stores map par dekho, retailer se seedhe chat karo
✓ Diabetes ki dawai khatam? Raat mein konsa store open hai — dekho. Emergency ka saathi
