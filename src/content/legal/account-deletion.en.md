# Account Deletion Policy

*Last Updated: 18 May 2026*

*Status: Baseline content — pending legal review*

This policy explains how you can delete your Dukanchi account, what happens after you request deletion, how long you have to change your mind, and what data we keep afterwards. It supports your right to erasure under the DPDP Act 2023.

## 1. How to Delete Your Account

The easiest way to delete your account is from within the app:

> **Profile → Delete Account → confirm**

You may also be asked for an optional reason for leaving (up to 500 characters). Giving a reason is not required.

If for any reason you cannot use the in-app option, you may email us at dukanchiapp@gmail.com with the subject line **"Account Deletion Request"**, using the phone number or email registered on your Dukanchi account so we can identify you.

## 2. What Happens When You Request Deletion

When you request deletion, we begin a **soft-delete** process. In one atomic step:

- We record the **date and time of your request** and calculate your **deletion date** (30 days later).
- We store your optional reason, if you gave one.
- Your profile, listings, and photos are **hidden from public view immediately** — other users can no longer see them.
- Your **push-notification channels are removed immediately**, so notifications stop right away.

Your account is now in a deletion grace window.

## 3. Grace Window

After you request deletion, there is a **30-day grace window**.

During these 30 days, you can change your mind and **restore your account**. Restoring cancels the deletion: your profile, listings, and photos become visible again, and your account works as normal. You can restore from within the app while the grace window is active.

If you do nothing, the grace window simply runs its course.

## 4. Permanent Deletion Timeline

Within 30 days, in line with the DPDP Act 2023, your personal data is permanently removed, except for records we must keep by law.

Once the 30-day grace window ends, your account is treated as **expired**. It can no longer be restored and is permanently inaccessible.

## 5. What We Keep After Permanent Deletion

Even after permanent deletion, we may keep a small amount of data where the law requires or allows it:

- **Records we are legally required to retain**, such as data needed for audit or to handle a dispute.
- A **legal-consent audit trail** showing that consent was given and later withdrawn.
- **Aggregated analytics** that contain no personal identifiers and cannot be linked back to you.

We keep this data only for as long as the law requires.

## 6. What Cannot Be Recovered

Once the 30-day grace window has ended and permanent deletion has taken place, **nothing can be recovered**. Your profile, listings, photos, chats, and other personal data are gone. If you wish to use Dukanchi again afterwards, you would need to create a new account.

## 7. Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or about the data we keep, please contact our Grievance Officer. Details are on the [Grievance Officer page](/legal/grievance).
